Women's Revolt™ was born as a literary series after more than 20 years of development. Today it is much more: a global brand spanning publishing, television, animation, fashion, sport, cinema, travel, gaming and startups.

Published by Micronos Ltd, distributed worldwide, with full creative control and intellectual property ownership.
