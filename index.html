WOMEN'S REVOLT™ — THE MANIFESTO
La Rivolta delle Donne
The Revolt did not begin today.
It began twenty years ago, in silence.
Like all serious things.
We do not ask.
We do not wait.
We do not explain.
We are the women who already knew.
Who saw before the others.
Who stopped waiting for permission.
The Revolt is not a protest.
It is not a petition.
It is not a hashtag.

It is a system.
Built for the one who is alone in a room.
For those who cannot afford to wait.
We do not console. We act.
We do not ask for permission. We declare.
We do not explain. We affirm.
No one is left out.
Period.
This is the Circle.
This is the Signal.
This is the Revolt.
